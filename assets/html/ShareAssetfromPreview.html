  1. Preview Assets

Share Asset from Preview

You have total rights to a secure asset distribution. With appropriate permissions, Email the asset link directly to the recipient. The link is shared as it can be easily accessed when compared to an attachment with a large file size.

Email the asset:

  • Click    to mail the asset link


  • Enter the recipient’s email id in the ‘Email’ field
  • Enter the subject in the ‘Subject’ field
  • Select the asset versions to be shared
  • Enter the message in the ‘Message’ field
  • Click the ‘SEND FILES’ button to send the email. To cancel the entered data, click the ‘CANCEL’ button.
  • Once the mail is sent, a successful message displays

Note: As opposed to the actual asset output files being sent as attachments in the email, a link to the asset file will be shared, which will be accessible for 7 days post which it will expire.
